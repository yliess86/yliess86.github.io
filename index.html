Who Am I?

I am a PhD Student at the De Vinci Innovation Center in the Human-Computer Interaction group.

In my research, I explore the use of Artificial Intelligence, and expecially its subfield Deep Learning, to develop new kinds of interactions, and/or experiences.

I am convinced that Open Source and Open Science is the way to go. As many projects tend to stay in a Proof of Concept stage, I always try to make them almost production-ready.

I also have a strong interest in Computer Graphics, Video Games Dev, and Digital Drawing.
